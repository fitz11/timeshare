# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 2026
**Last Updated:** January 2026

## Introduction

Timeshare ("we", "our", or "the app") is a calendar-sharing application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

By using Timeshare, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

| Data Type | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| **Email Address** | Account creation, login, and user identification |
| **Display Name** | Showing your name to other users you share calendars with |
| **Profile Photo** | Optional profile customization (via Google Sign-In) |
| **Calendar Data** | Names of calendars you create |
| **Event Data** | Event names, dates, times, colors, shapes, and recurrence patterns |
| **Friends List** | User IDs of people you've added as friends |

### Information Collected Automatically

| Data Type | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| **Crash Reports** | App stability improvement via Firebase Crashlytics |
| **Device Information** | Debugging and crash report context |
| **Usage Analytics** | Understanding app usage patterns (if enabled) |

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide the Service** - Store and sync your calendars and events
2. **Enable Sharing** - Allow you to share calendars with friends
3. **Improve the App** - Analyze crash reports to fix bugs
4. **Communicate** - Send service-related notifications (if applicable)

We do **not** use your information to:

- Sell to third parties
- Target advertisements
- Build marketing profiles
- Share with data brokers

## Data Storage and Security

### Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored in **Firebase Firestore**, operated by Google. Data is primarily stored in Google's data centers in the United States.

### Security Measures

- Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
- Data is encrypted at rest by Google's infrastructure
- Access to data is controlled by Firebase Security Rules
- Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication

### Important Limitation

Your calendar and event data is **not end-to-end encrypted**. This means the data can be accessed by database administrators. See [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for details.

## Data Sharing

Your data is shared only with:

1. **Users You Choose** - People you explicitly share calendars with
2. **Service Providers** - Google/Firebase for infrastructure
3. **Legal Requirements** - If required by law

We do not sell your personal data.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

### Access Your Data
You can view all your data within the app (calendars, events, profile).

### Delete Your Data
You can delete your account at any time through the app's profile settings. This will:
- Delete all calendars you own
- Delete all events in your calendars
- Remove your user profile
- Remove you from others' friends lists

### Export Your Data
Contact us to request an export of your data.

### Correct Your Data
You can update your display name and other profile information in the app.

## Data Retention

- Your data is retained as long as your account exists
- Deleted data is removed from our systems within 30 days
- Backup copies may persist for up to 90 days before automatic deletion

## Children's Privacy

Timeshare is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.
